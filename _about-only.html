That lesson never left me.

So I went and earned the training to do it right — a Bachelor of Business Administration, an MBA, and a Master of Science in Business Analytics, all from The University of Iowa — plus finance certificates in Finance, Financial Decision-Making, and Investment Management, with an MS in Finance underway.

Now I bring the whole stack to small and mid-sized companies: the operating judgment to know which questions matter, the analytics to answer them, and the engineering to ship the tool that acts on the answer — one person, start to finish. That's the entire point of Work Smarter Analytics.
